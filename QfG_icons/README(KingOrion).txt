To install these icons RIGHT CLICK on a shortcut and
click PROPERTIES.  Click on the SHORTCUTS tab, then 
on the CHANGE ICON button.  Then click on BROWSE, and
change the FILES OF TYPE selection to ALL FILES, then 
go to the folder this file was extracted to and click
OPEN.  Select the icon you want, click OK, then OK 
again, and you're finished!  Enjoy!
