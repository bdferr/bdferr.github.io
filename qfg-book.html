Questing for Glory:
The Definitive Tale of Quest for Glory

by Lori Cole, Corey Cole, and Jason Mical.
Questing for Glory looks at the creative and technical development of the games, the narrative, the fantasy elements, the art, the music, the world of Gloriana, and Quest for Glory's legacy and impact on computer RPGs. We'll also include QfG-inspired games like Hero-U. Basically it will cover everything a Quest for Glory fan would ever want to know and more!
